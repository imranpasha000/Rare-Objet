Supply Chain Policy

Our commitment to ethical and sustainable supply chain practices

Our Policy Framework

Just A Click is committed to maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct, environmental responsibility, and social accountability throughout our supply chain. Our policies ensure that all suppliers meet our rigorous standards for quality, sustainability, and ethical practices.

Code of Conduct

All suppliers must adhere to our comprehensive code of conduct covering labor practices, environmental standards, and business ethics.

Environmental Standards

Suppliers must demonstrate commitment to environmental protection, waste reduction, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Labor Standards

We require suppliers to maintain fair labor practices, provide safe working conditions, and respect human rights.

Compliance Requirements

Quality Certification

ISO 9001:2015 quality management system certification required.

Environmental Certification

ISO 14001:2015 environmental management system certification preferred.

Social Compliance

SA8000 or equivalent social accountability certification required.

Security Standards

C-TPAT or equivalent supply chain security certification required.
